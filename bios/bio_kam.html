Graphic Designer
Kam Sullivan

Kameron has over 12 years experience as a graphic designer in meeting & events.  In 2000 he became lead designer for a meeting planning company in Orlando, FL.  There he designed print, web, and multimedia projects for a wide range of clients and industries.  He also worked as a freelance designer for many years, until in 2010 he formed his own design agency to better serve a growing client base.  In early 2011, Kameron joined the Kubeworks team.
