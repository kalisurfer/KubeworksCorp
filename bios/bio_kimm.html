Project Manager
Kimm Henry

Kimm has been in the Travel/Hospitality Industry for over 15 years. Her career began with the airlines which gave her the opportunity to travel around the world. After nearly 3 years of being a flight attendant, she grew tired of a life on the road. But the travel bug had bitten and she wanted to stay within the industry so she took a job with a major hotel chain as a conference service manager. In the late 90's Kimm jumped from the hotel side of the business to the corporate side, by becoming a meeting planner for a large travel company. It is here where Kimm met Lisa Scott and become experienced with various meeting planning software. With raising a family, once again life on the road as a planner became hectic, so it was a perfect fit when Lisa offered her an opportunity with Kubeworks. Kimm is a transplanted New Yorker and currently resides in the southern laid-back city of Richmond, Virginia.
