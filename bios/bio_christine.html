Project Manager
Christine Doyle

Christine Doyle, CMP has over ten years of experience in the Meetings and Hospitality Industry.  She is a designated Certified Meeting Planner (CMP) and an active member of Meeting Professionals International (MPI). Christine's experience includes managing logistics and all aspects of meetings ranging from 10 to 10,000 attendees which includes site sourcing, contract negotiation, creating/monitoring client budgets, VIP coordination, transportation management, food and beverage planning, speaker coordination, agenda/itinerary management (design, coordination and production), audio/visual coordination, final billing reconciliation, registration website creation and maintenance.
