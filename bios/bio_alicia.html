Alicia Boyle

Alicia Boyle began her career in the Meetings and Events Industry in 1999 as Training and Development Manager with a top third party meeting logistic company. Alicia spearheaded the implementation of an on-line registration system and developed the SOPs for the company to transition from a manual registration process to an automated process. Her processes streamlined the procedures for registration and led the company to successfully and efficiently plan hundreds of meetings a year, including two 10,000 person meetings. Alicia has a keen ability to problem solve and identify solutions which contributes to her success in website development and attendee management.
